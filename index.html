Advocacy & Activism

I'm always open to doing volunteer mapping and data analysis work for nonprofits, grassroots organizations, and progressive political groups and candidates.

I have experience organizing for fossil fuel divestment and climate justice, as well as advocacy related to voting rights and electoral politics. I am also keenly interested in learning about what you're working on. If you have a project in mind, let's chat.

Urban Geography

I'm an urban geographer by training and at heart. I welcome any chance to work on research projects (qualitative and quantitative alike) that revolve around cities, particularly non-car transportation, climate justice, housing and displacement, and political power.

My undergraduate honors thesis explored the accessibility and mobility of the non-car transportation system in St. Louis, MO.

The Science of Where

I help federal government organizations solve complex problems as a member of Esri's National Government Geospatial Center. My projects span the entire ArcGIS platform; reach out if you have questions or would like to hear about how location intelligence can help overcome your organizational and analytical challenges.
